Friends: the reunion

Over the past 17 years, the details of the classic series final could have been forgotten.To the release of the Friends the reunion let`s remember how the original series ended.

Ross and Rachel finally together

The story of the relationship between Ross Geller (David Schwimmer) and Rachel Green (Jennifer Aniston) began in the first episode of "Friends" and logically ended in the last - 236. For 10 years, the heroes hid mutual sympathy, confessed their love, parted, hurt each other and even hated each other. In the final, however, they still ended up together In the eighth season, Rachel confessed to friends that she was pregnant, but refused to reveal the name of the child's father. It turned out to be Ross. At the end of the season, Rachel and Ross had a daughter, Emma. The heroes converged again in the final episode of the series: Rachel decided to leave her new job in Paris and stay in New York with Ross.

Monica and Chandler adopted twins

For the first 4 seasons, Monica Geller (Courteney Cox) and Chandler Bing (Matthew Perry) were separately looking for life partners. Chandler dated a strange girl Janice (Maggie Wheeler), and Monica met eye doctor Richard (Tom Selleck), a friend of her parents, But Janice cheated on Chandler with her ex-husband, and Richard made it clear that he did not want to have children, and for Monica this became a reason to disperse. In the fifth season during the trip of friends to Ross and Emily's wedding in London, they woke up in the same bed. Chandler and Monica decided not to be limited to one night and continued their relationship, and then began to live together. In the seventh season, they got married and decided to adopt. The couple decided to become parents for the child of a young girl Erica (Anna Faris) from Ohio, who accidentally became pregnant and was not ready to become a single mother. Erica gave birth in the last episode of season 10 - however, not one child, but twins, which came as a surprise to everyone, including Erica herself. In the finale, they moved from an apartment in New York to a large house in the suburbs.

Phoebe married Mike

The story of Phoebe Buffet (Lisa Kudrow) was full of tragic moments, despite this she maintained a positive attitude and was one of the funniest in the company of friends. Last season she found her by accepting a marriage proposal from Mike Hannigan (Paul Rudd) - a guy from a rich family, whom Joey accidentally introduced to Phoebe, who forgot to find a mate for Phoebe during their double date. It was marriage that played an important role in the history of the heroine. In season 10, she finally found a loved one and was able to forget about her difficult youth.

Joey Tribbiani aka dr. Drake Ramorey

Joey Tribbiani (Matt LeBlanc) dreamed of becoming a famous actor. And if Joey did not have a stable relationship by the end of Friends, then his career did work out. After that, Joey managed to star in several feature films and small series.
